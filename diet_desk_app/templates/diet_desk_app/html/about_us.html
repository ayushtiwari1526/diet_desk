Our Story

We're on a mission to make
nutrition accessible for all

DietDesk was founded in 2022 by a team of nutritionists and tech enthusiasts who believed that good health shouldn't be a luxury.

Building a healthier India, one meal at a time

At DietDesk, we bridge the gap between people and certified dietitians. Our platform makes it easy to get personalised, science-backed nutrition advice without the high costs and long waits of traditional consultations.

We understand that every person is unique — their body, lifestyle, food preferences, and health goals differ. That's why every plan on DietDesk is crafted individually, not generated from a template.

  • Personalised plans for 20+ health conditions
  • Plans available in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu & more
  • Affordable pricing starting at ₹499/month
  • 12,000+ satisfied users since launch
